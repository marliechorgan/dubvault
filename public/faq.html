FAQ

How do I submit a track?

Log in, hit Submit, and upload your track. You’ll get £25–£50 upfront plus a revenue cut.

What’s the subscription deal?

Basic (£10/month): 3 tracks weekly, voting, ad-free, DubPoints. Premium (£15/month): Adds early access, exclusive content, merch.

How do DubPoints work?

Earn ‘em by subscribing, sharing tracks, or referring mates. Cash ‘em in for merch or free months.

Merch and workshops?

Merch drops in 2026—hoodies, vinyl, gear. Workshops start Q4 2025 for premium users (£50–£100).
