Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I submit a track?

Log in to your account and navigate to the Submit page. Fill out the form with your track details and upload the audio file along with optional artwork.

2. How do I download tracks?

Visit the Tracks page to browse available tracks. Click on the "Download Track" link next to the track you want to download.

3. Do I need a paid subscription to access tracks?

Yes, you need to have an active subscription to download exclusive tracks. You can subscribe via the Profile page.
