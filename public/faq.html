Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download tracks?

You must be logged in with a paid subscription. If you’re not subscribed, head to My Profile to upgrade. Once subscribed, you can click the “Download” button on any track that hasn’t expired.

How long are tracks available?

Each new dub is available for 2 months. After that, it expires and can’t be downloaded. We keep the platform fresh with weekly additions, so there’s always something new.

How do I cancel my subscription?

If you’re a paying member, you’ll see a “Cancel Subscription” button on the My Profile page. In the future, we plan to offer a direct link to the Stripe Billing Portal so you can manage your subscription instantly.

Is it really £20/month?

Yes. We’re priced at £20/month to ensure fair compensation for producers (we pay £200/track) and to keep the platform sustainable while growing toward our goal of 50,000 members by Year 2.

Can I share the dubs with my friends?

We encourage you to DJ and play the tracks at events, but we ask you not to distribute them outside of DubVault. Each file is watermarked, and we rely on your integrity to support our producers and the UK underground scene.
