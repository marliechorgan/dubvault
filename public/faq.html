Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I submit a track?

Log in and navigate to the Submit page. Fill out the form with your track details and upload your audio file along with optional artwork.

2. How do I download tracks?

Visit the Tracks page to browse available tracks and click the "Download Track" button next to the track you want.

3. Do I need a subscription to access tracks?

Yes, an active subscription is required. Check your status on your My Profile page.

4. How do I contact support?

For any queries or issues, please email support@dubvault.co.uk.
