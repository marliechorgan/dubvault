Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a track?

Log in and go to Submit. Upload your track and artwork—artists get £25–£50 upfront plus revenue share.

What’s included in the subscription?

Basic (£10/month): 3 weekly tracks, voting, ad-free, DubPoints. Premium (£15/month): Adds early access, exclusive content, and merch drops.

How do I earn DubPoints?

Earn points by subscribing, sharing tracks, or referring friends—redeem for merch or free months.

When’s the merch store launching?

Year 2 (2026)—stay tuned for hoodies, vinyl, and more!
