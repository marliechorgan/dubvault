DubVault Business Plan

1. Executive Summary

Concept
DubVault is a UK-based subscription platform offering exclusive, unreleased “dubs” focusing on community-driven curation over algorithmic control.

Growth Target
1) End of Year 1: ~3,000–4,000 users
2) End of Year 2: 10,000 users total
3) End of Year 3: Over 20,000, with new expansions in licensing & events

2. Market Analysis

Major platforms overshadow smaller genres with mainstream hits. We target the gap in niche, underground music, giving fans & producers direct influence.

3. Product & Offering

Subscriptions at £20/month, producers gain revenue shares plus an upfront. We host exclusive content locked behind real community validation.

4. Marketing & Growth Strategy

Emphasize local clubs, student events, influencer DJs, and viral user-shared content rather than corporate ad funnels. Fans become ambassadors through our DubPoints system.

5. Operations & Platform

Lean dev team with a focus on performance. Partnerships with local record shops, pressing plants for future vinyl expansions, and minimal overhead for maximum agility.

6. Financial Model

Each subscription generates monthly recurring revenue. Producer payouts remain fair and create loyalty. Workshops, merch, and licensing add multiple revenue streams.

7. Roadmap to 10,000 Subscribers

Months 1–3: Beta invites, grassroots marketing
Months 3–6: Expand influencer ties, small festival presences
Months 6–12: Launch major marketing push with city-based events

8. Keys to Success

Authenticity, scene-centric marketing, and a community that invests in—and is invested by—the platform. No hidden throttling or pay-to-play for exposure.

Fast-Track Timeline (6 Months to Scale)

Hardcore push in local DJ circuits, synergy with youth events and radio shows, leveraging user-submitted success stories to entice new signups.

Marketing Angles & Sample Lines

“From the people, for the people.”
“Underground music deserves an underground champion.”
“Your sound, your choice—forget the algorithmic gatekeepers.”

Interviews with Producers

Showcase Q&As, talk production tips, and share them across social media, highlighting the artist’s authenticity and direct fan interaction.

Encouragement & Practical Mindset Tips

“Remember Your Why,” “Celebrate Each Milestone,” “Stay Real,” and “Champion Others.” Passion fuels progress; authenticity cements loyalty.

End of Business Plan.
