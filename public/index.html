About DubVault

Our Mission

We’re not just a platform—we’re a rebellion. DubVault’s built to amplify independent producers, pumping out raw Drum & Bass, Speed Garage, and House that hits different. This is where the underground lives, unshackled and untamed.

Why We Fight

The mainstream’s a racket—starving artists with scraps while algorithms choke out the real shit. We’re here to flip that: upfront cash, revenue cuts, and community votes that let the streets decide what slaps.
