About DubVault

DubVault is a UK-based platform specializing in exclusive, unreleased “dubs” tailored for the underground dance music scene. We focus on supporting local producers,

Our mission is simple: provide a steady pipeline of 2 fresh dubs every week, exclusively available on DubVault for at least two months. This gives our subscribers a unique edge—early access to tunes that remain unheard on mainstream platforms.

Above all, we value community. By giving local producers a direct line to DJs seeking fresh, exclusive sounds, we keep underground dance music thriving.

How Producers & DJs Benefit

Producers

• Earn direct payouts for each dub.
• Get early, exclusive exposure to our passionate audience.
• Connect directly with DJs who crave new underground sounds.

DJs

• Unlock unreleased tracks before the mainstream.
• Keep your sets one step ahead with exclusive basslines.
• Build your reputation on truly unique, fresh tunes.

Our Commitment

We aim to foster a genuine connection between artists, DJs, and fans. Our platform embraces a non-corporate vibe, emphasizing the raw, gritty energy of the UK’s underground dance floors. With fair payouts to producers and exclusive benefits to DJs, DubVault ensures everyone in the community wins.
