About DubVault

“Where the Underground Thrives”

Our Vision

DubVault's a war machine for the underground UK dance scene—Drum & Bass, Speed Garage, House. We're here to empower indie producers, arm DJs with exclusive cuts, and hook fans into a raw ecosystem that's free from mainstream sludge. Profit's the goal, and we're gunning for it fast.

Our Mission

One platform, no compromise: producers cash out fairly with £25–£50 upfront plus revenue shares, DJs snag exclusive bangers, and fans steer the ship with votes—zero algorithms. We're scaling quick, stacking revenue, and keeping it real.

Why Join?

Artists: instant pay, keep your rights, six-month exclusivity, and promo juice—Q&As, remix contests, real fans. Fans: vote tracks up, earn DubPoints, snag merch. DJs: fresh cuts nobody else has. Mainstream's a rip-off; we're the antidote.

The Hustle

Year 1: £171K profit, 2K subs. Year 2: £513K, merch and workshops kick in. Year 3: £1.1M, licensing marketplace drops. Viral TikTok, referral cash, and London DJ USBs fuel the fire. We're not just saving the scene—we're banking it.
