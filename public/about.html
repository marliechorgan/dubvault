About DubVault

DubVault is the heart of underground dance music. We empower independent producers, give top DJs exclusive access to fresh tracks, and nurture a community built on genuine passion.

Our artist-first philosophy ensures that creators receive fair compensation and maintain full ownership of their work. Tracks rise organically through community votes—not algorithms—ensuring that quality and authenticity prevail.

We also celebrate the rich history of genres like Drum & Bass, Speed Garage, and House, preserving their essence in every release.

Our Core Values

Cultural Preservation

We stand for keeping the spirit of underground music alive.

Fair Compensation

Every artist is rewarded upfront, ensuring sustainability and creative freedom.

Community Driven

Our platform is built by and for music lovers, where every vote matters.
