Our Mission

We’re here to empower independent producers and keep the raw, real sound of UK underground dance music—Drum & Bass, Speed Garage, House—alive and kicking.

Why We Exist

Mainstream platforms screw over artists with pennies in royalties and bury tracks under algorithms. DubVault says nah—pay producers upfront, share the revenue, and let the community decide what slaps. No corporate playlists, just pure underground fire.

What We Stand For

Artist-First

Cash upfront, full ownership, real fans.

Culture Matters

Preserving the beats that built the scene.

Community Power

You vote, you shape the sound—no suits allowed.

What’s Next

We’re cooking a custom web-app for offline listening (Q3 2025), a merch store with vinyl and tees (2026), and workshops with scene legends (Q4 2025). The underground’s just getting started.
