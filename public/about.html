About DubVault

DubVault is a UK-based platform specializing in exclusive, unreleased “dubs” tailored for the underground dance music scene. We focus on supporting local producers.

Our mission is simple: provide a steady pipeline of 2 fresh dubs every week, exclusively available on DubVault for at least two months. This gives our subscribers a unique edge—early access to tunes that remain unheard on mainstream platforms.

Above all, we value community. By giving local producers a direct line to DJs seeking fresh, exclusive sounds, we keep underground dance music thriving.

How Producers & DJs Benefit

For Producers

Get your music heard by top DJs and gain exposure in the underground scene.

For DJs

Access exclusive, unreleased tracks to keep your sets fresh and unique.
