About DubVault

DubVault is the heartbeat of underground UK dance music. We empower independent producers, connect top DJs with exclusive tracks, and foster a passionate community free from mainstream influence.

Our mission is to create a platform where artists earn fairly through upfront payments and revenue sharing, retaining full ownership, while fans drive curation with organic votes—algorithm-free.

We preserve the soul of genres like Drum & Bass, Speed Garage, and House, ensuring the underground thrives.

Our Vision

Artist-First

Immediate £25–£50 payments per track, plus a share of revenue based on plays.

Cultural Impact

Protecting UK underground sounds from dilution.

Community Driven

Your votes shape the platform—no corporate playlists.
